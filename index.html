How do we help you?

HARO is great for receiving requests from journalists that might want to feature you, but it's a lot of work to scan all the requests and do it quickly to respond within deadlines. Our CEO used to spend 5 minutes scanning each email. At 3 emails a day, that’s almost 8 hours a month!

 

Our service team at MonitorHARO helps you by selecting the perfect requests for you to pitch, and saves you time by emailing you only when we find requests that are a good match. We scan not just the subject line of the requests, but the full description of every request, to make sure we find all the right matches for you.

We use artificial intelligence to help us spot appropriate matches, but we believe a human touch is needed to ensure we never, ever waste your time by sending you requests that might not be a good match. This is why every email we send you is crafted by a human.

When you sign up, we guide you through a simple setup process where we learn about you or your company, what you would like to be known for in the media, and which keywords- might be good indicators for a match. We gather plenty of information to make sure we can find just the right matches for you, three times a day, every day.

Each month of service is paid in advance, but to make sure you're happy, we offer a 100% money back guarantee, no questions asked, on your last payment.
